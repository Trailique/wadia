How To Stay Healthy

Maintain a Balanced diet

Eat a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats. Limit intake of processed foods, sugary beverages, and excessive amounts of red or processed meats. Stay hydrated by drinking an adequate amount of water throughout the day.

Exercise Regularly

Engage in regular physical activity, including aerobic exercises, strength training, and flexibility exercises. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic activity per week, along with muscle-strengthening activities on two or more days per week.

Don't Stress much!!!

Practice stress-reducing techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, yoga, or mindfulness. Take breaks and engage in activities you enjoy to help manage stress.
